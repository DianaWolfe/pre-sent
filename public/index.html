What is this?

pre.SENT is a living art gallery that generates original artwork in real time. Move the slider to travel through six psychological eras of one life, from the cartoonish sarcasm of childhood through the confessional line work of crisis to the earned sovereignty of now. Each position generates a new image using DALL-E 3, guided by the emotional and aesthetic DNA of that life stage.

About the Artist

Diana Wolfe, PhD is an AI researcher, strategist, and builder whose work sits at the intersection of human psychology and machine intelligence. pre.SENT is part of an ongoing series of experiments in applied AI, exploring what generative systems can make when given a specific human psychology to work from.
