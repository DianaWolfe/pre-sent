What is this?

pre.SENT is AI performance art trained on the visual DNA of a real artist. It traces one woman's life through six psychological eras, from the cartoonish sarcasm of a child who moved seventeen times to the refined storybook sovereignty of the artist she became. Every image is generated in real time by gpt-image-1, guided by the actual aesthetic and emotional fingerprint of each period. The AI does not invent. It inherits.

About the Artist

Diana Wolfe, PhD is an AI researcher, strategist, and artist. pre.SENT is one experiment in a larger question: what happens when a generative system is given not a style to imitate but a life to understand? The visual arc here, from childhood wonder to illustrative maturity to refined personal mythology, is real. The AI did not invent it. It was handed it.
